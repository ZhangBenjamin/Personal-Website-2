About Me

I have started coding at my junior year of high school, learning C and Python and this was the first time I enjoyed coding and problem solving, even though I wasnt the best at it during that time. I am fascinated in all parts of computer science, but I enjoy web development the most, specifically the backend side of it because I love working with databases of all forms and I am currently studying cloud computing and AWS.
