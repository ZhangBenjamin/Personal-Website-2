About Me

I am curently a senior at Hunter College, majoring in computer science and minoring in mathematics. I have started coding at my junior year of high school, learning C and Python and this was the first time I enjoyed coding and problem solving, even though I wasnt the best at it during that time. I officially thought of doing computer science as a career was in my junior year of college becuase in the past I was studying mathematics at that time and I thought of computer science as a hobby at that time. I wasn't too sure what I really wanted to do with mathematics as a career in five years time and remembered that I really enjoyed computer science and I switched majors.

I am fascinated in all parts of computer science, but I enjoy web development the most, specifically the backend side of it because I love working with databases of all forms and I am currently studying cloud computing and AWS.
